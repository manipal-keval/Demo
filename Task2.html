New York City

Statue of Liberty — Iconic symbol of freedom and a historic landmark welcoming visitors since 1886.
Times Square — A vibrant commercial and entertainment hub, famous for its flashing billboards and crowds.
Wall Street — Historic financial district and the center of American finance and trading.
Central Park — Expansive urban park offering lakes, trails, and cultural attractions.
Columbus Circle — Major traffic circle and landmark marking the southwest corner of Central Park.
Brooklyn Bridge — Iconic suspension bridge connecting Manhattan and Brooklyn with panoramic views.
Empire State Building — Renowned Art Deco skyscraper offering observation decks and city views.
One World Trade Center — Tallest building in the Western Hemisphere, built at the World Trade Center site.
Chrysler Building — Elegant Art Deco tower famous for its gleaming terraced crown.
The Edge — Outdoor observation deck at Hudson Yards with dramatic city views.
Lincoln Center — Major cultural complex hosting music, opera, and dance performances.
SoHo — Neighborhood known for boutiques, galleries, and cast-iron architecture.
